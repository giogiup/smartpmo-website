About SmartPMO.ai

Built to solve a problem no one else was solving.

The Problem

I run enterprise PMO operations. I'm responsible for implementing AI at scale across organisations and within PMO functions. Despite 15+ years in this work, I was drowning in content.

Subscribed to 50+ AI sources. Still missed critical developments at the PMO-AI intersection.

8.5 million articles and blog posts are published online every day — approximately 7.5 million blog posts (Tech Business News, 2025) plus nearly 1 million news articles from 26,000+ publishers (NewsCatcher, 2024). Finding the fraction of a percent relevant to PMO work is impossible manually.

I needed a system that could process millions of articles, score them for PMO applicability, and surface the signal. So I built one.

Who Built This

I'm a PMO professional who came up through the full delivery chain:

Software Engineering → Business Analysis → System Design → Project Management → Programme Management → PMO Leadership

Over 15 years I've designed PMO processes from scratch, restructured individual PMOs, rebuilt enterprise PMO functions across multiple divisions, and currently oversee enterprise PMOs at scale.

I'm also an active AI practitioner. I work with AI daily — not as a casual user. I'm actively forging enterprise AI strategy and developing AI-powered ecosystems in organisations.

That combination — deep PMO domain knowledge plus hands-on AI implementation — is what drives this. I built SmartPMO.ai because I needed it. I'm sharing it because every PMO professional has the same problem.

How It Works

SmartPMO.ai runs an automated AI pipeline daily. It scans hundreds of sources, scores every article for PMO relevance, and surfaces the ones worth your time.

Discover → PreFilter → Score → Fetch → Enrich → Publish

The engine processes RSS feeds, news APIs, and targeted searches. AI models evaluate each article for PMO applicability — both direct mentions and inferred relevance. The highest-scoring articles are enriched with summaries and categorisation, then published to the site.

The details of how the engine works are proprietary. The results speak for themselves.

Three Article Categories

PMO Related

AI tools and techniques explicitly for PMO work. Mentions project management, portfolio management, resource allocation, or risk management alongside AI capabilities.

AI General
